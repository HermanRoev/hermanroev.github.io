About us!

Ottimo is a place to spend time with those closest to you. Time of laughter and love. We offer a unique journey through our menu, filled with our love and passion for food, so that our guests have a fantastic experience. An experience topped off with spotless service and quality.

Wedding

Congratulations on your marriage!

The wedding day is an important day, so it is important to know where it will be held or celebrated. Here at Ottimo, we are happy to prepare a venue so that you can have a fantastic celebration.

Our premises are furnished with a romantic atmosphere, with a capacity of up to 80 guests.

The Ottimo team will help you create an atmospheric celebration, and have no problem accommodating your wishes.

Food and drink serving is included in our menu. In addition, there is access to alcohol service.

We wish you a wonderful marriage!

Closed event

Do you need a place to hold an event, including a summer party, Christmas party, dining out for large groups or a birthday?

Ottimo has the perfect venue, so you can hold an atmospheric meeting with close friends, family and, possibly, colleagues.

Our premises have a capacity of up to 50 people. Food and drink service, in addition to alcohol service, is available by choice from our menu and bar. The Ottimo team will be happy to help you satisfy your wishes.
